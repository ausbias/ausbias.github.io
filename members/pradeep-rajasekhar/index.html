Pradeep is a bioimage analyst at WEHI. He has a background in engineering and a PhD in Drug Discovery Biology (2017). Pradeep is a lead developer of two open-source software tools: napari-lattice and Gut Analysis Toolbox. He loves working across disciplines, particularly multimodal datasets. It has all come to the point where he is getting equally comfortable working with large tables or images!! :)
