Raeesah Hayatudin

Affiliation: Monash Biomedicine Discovery Institute

Role: PhD student

Website: https://www.linkedin.com/in/raeesah-hayatudin/

Raeesah is a final year PhD candidate at Monash University supervised by Dr Alexander Combes and Dr Juan Nunez-Iglesias. Her research is focused on optimising culture techniques, live imaging and volumetric image analysis pipelines within the field of kidney development. Her publications include papers in Frontiers in Molecular Biosciences and npj Metabolic Health and Disease. She has co-supervised Honours and undergraduate students, and currently holds a position as Teaching Associate for Monash University.

Interests

  • live imaging
  • tissue explant culture
  • image segmentation and tracking

Specialities

  • spinning disk confocal microscopy
  • fluorescence microscopy
  • napari/CellProfiler (fluent)
  • Fiji/Imaris (proficient)
  • Python (writing scripts with others' code)
