Ausbias now as a YouTube Channel

We’re excited to announce that Ausbias is now on YouTube!

What to Expect

Our new YouTube channel will host recordings of our monthly meetings, kicking off with the February 2026 presentation by the one and only Cameron Nowell from Monash Institute of Pharmaceutical Medicine. Once we have a few more recordings up I’ll create an index page here and maybe start embedding them or something. Note that it will be up to the individual speakers to decide whether they’re happy to have their presentation uploaded - sometimes we will host more closed meetings where sensitive data or topics may be discussed.

It’s also possible that in future we will host workshops or tutorials, article reviews, where to find the best coffee closest to a microscopy lab, and more.
